Our Subscription Boxes


We want to help you to discover new books and our subscription boxes are perfect for that.

Every month you will be sent a box with all sorts of goodies in it. These boxes are curated by our expert team with a theme in mind. We want to make reading an experience and bring the print publishing days back into fashion.

Do you have someone in your life who are readers but want to get them something different than the usual gift card? why not get them our subscription box. You can choose from a one off option to a yearly subscription.



Each box includes

  • The Book of the month
  • Mystery Book
  • Snacks
  • Tea or Coffee
  • Mug
  • Trinkets that match the theme


Cut off date is the 15th of each month for the next months box. Boxes will be shipped out on the first of each month.
